Our Services

Orthopedic Conditions

Comprehensive treatment for all orthopedic problems affecting bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons.

Vertigo & Balance

Specialized treatment for vertigo and balance problems using evidence-based techniques.

TMJ Dysfunction

Expert treatment for temporomandibular joint dysfunction to reduce pain and restore function.

Dry Needling

Advanced dry needling techniques to relieve muscle tension, reduce pain, and improve mobility.

What to Expect

1

Initial Evaluation

Your first visit consists mainly of a comprehensive evaluation where we address your problems and concerns. We will also explain your plan and goals for your physical therapy. This visit usually lasts a little longer than an hour to make sure we can check everything out.

2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your evaluation, we develop a customized treatment plan tailored to your specific goals. Your plan may include manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, education, and home programs.

3

One-on-One Sessions

Each session is dedicated entirely to you. Our therapists provide hands-on treatment, guide you through exercises, and adjust your plan as you progress toward your goals.

4

Unlock Your Hero

As you progress, we celebrate your victories and continue to challenge you. Our goal is to help you unlock the hidden hero inside and return to your activities stronger and more confident than ever.
